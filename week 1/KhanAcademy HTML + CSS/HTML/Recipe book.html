Cauliflower And Chicken Curry

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: kitchen
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken 300 g
Flour 100 g
Curry 30 g

Step 1: Cut the chicken thighs into bite-size pieces and cauliflower into florets.

Step 2: dd chicken & cauliflower, cook for 10 minutes until both the ingredients are soft and well-cooked.
